Intended Purpose
#1: Through the interactivites on the website, you are able to reflect on and learn about yourself/habits
#2: Learn to not immediately internalize everything and love yourself
#3: Inspire users to start learning to change their mindset


Interactivity Description
Bullet Journal Write down your thoughts and use the bullet jounal space as a place to rant and share out your opinion about anything you want to get out.
Choose Your Adventure Game The Choose your Adventure Game will have a positive story path and a negative story path to inform others about the impact of beauty standards on mental health.
Educational Learn more about how Beauty Standards are causing negativity and impacting day to day life for individuals.
Visualizing Data & Creating Graphs Graphs and charts are used to track your progress and feeling throughout the day.
